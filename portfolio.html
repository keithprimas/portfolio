What's Front-End Deployment?

"What's Front-End Development?" is an engaging front-coding project designed to teach users the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. The platform employs a unique approach, blending visual examples with clear written descriptions to facilitate an immersive learning experience. Users can also test their knowledge of the material by taking quizzes at the end of each course. Additionally, the project features a progress tracking system, enabling learners to monitor and celebrate their achievements as they navigate the essentials of front-end development in a comprehensive and interactive manner.

  • CSS for homepage and content pages
  • Homepage content

Achieving optimal readability across devices required attention to text size, spacing, and layout. Balancing aesthetics with functionality became crucial to guarantee a positive user experience on both desktop and mobile platforms.

Bizi- A Fullstack Business Card Builder

"Bizi" is a fullstack application where users can create professional business cards through a seamless signup and login experience. Using technologies such as Handlebars.js, MySQL, Node.js, and Express.js, the platform ensures a robust and responsive web application. Users can efficiently generate personalized business cards by providing information through intuitive prompts.

My roles and tasks:
  • Project concept
  • CSS for login page and homepage
  • Signup and Login routes
  • Backend server javascript
  • Deployment to Heroku
Challenges:

    We encountered difficulties in establishing backend routes for our project, struggling at first to ensure proper connectivity and functionality. Integrating the login page with the MySQL database presented challenges as we worked to establish a working connection. Additionally, deploying our app on Heroku proved to be a complex task, demanding careful configuration and troubleshooting to ensure a successful deployment.
