About Me

Hi! My name is Keith Primas, and I am from Hickory, NC. Currently, I am living in Charlotte, NC, and I have been here and loving it for almost 4 years. I am the oldest of four and a proud uncle to my wonderful niece and nephew (pictured above). My coding journey began early in 2023, when I was looking for a new job and possibly a change in my field. I was working as a pharmacy technician for 4 years, but I saw no substantial growth in the future for that role. I wanted to go back to school and get my graduate degree in biology, but I did not want to accrue more student loans, so I started researching careers that I could see myself enjoying while also ensuring financial, professional, and personal growth. I came across software development one day and was immediately intrigued by the prospect. From there, I would look further into what careers software development could offer, what pathways and programs would be most practical, and what the current job prospects were for software development. I eventually landed on applying for and enrolling in the UNCC Fullstack Bootcamp, and I never looked back! My coding journey has only begun, but I'm excited to continue learning, practicing, and exploring software development!
